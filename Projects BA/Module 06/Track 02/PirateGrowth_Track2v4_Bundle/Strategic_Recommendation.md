### Slide 8 — Game Theory: don't enter the price war
**2-firm Nash equilibrium on penetration pricing**

|  | Them: Hold | Them: Cut 7% | Them: Cut 15% |
|---|---|---|---|
| Us: Hold | 17.50 / 17.50 | 16.41 / 18.00 | 14.99 / 18.21 |
| Us: Cut 7% | 18.00 / 16.41 | 17.02 / 17.02 | 15.75 / 17.36 |
| Us: Cut 15% | 18.21 / 14.99 | 17.36 / 15.75 | **16.24 / 16.24 ⭐ NASH** |

**Pareto-optimal (Hold/Hold):** £17.50M each. **Nash (Cut15/Cut15):** £16.24M each. **Loss to cooperation: £1.26M each per year.**

**Insight:** This is a prisoner's dilemma. Defect to virality — don't fight on price.
